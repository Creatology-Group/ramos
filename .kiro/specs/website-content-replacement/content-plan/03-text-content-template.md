## CONTENT WRITING TIPS

### Headlines:
- Start with action verbs or power words
- Focus on benefits, not features
- Create curiosity or urgency
- Keep it scannable and memorable

### Body Copy:
- Write in active voice
- Use short sentences and paragraphs
- Focus on "you" (user-centric)
- Include specific details and examples
- End with a clear next step

### CTAs (Call-to-Action):
- Use action verbs (Get, Start, Try, Join, etc.)
- Create urgency when appropriate
- Be specific about what happens next
- Keep it short and punchy

### Navigation & Labels:
- Use familiar, expected terms
- Be descriptive but concise
- Maintain consistency across site
- Consider user search intent
